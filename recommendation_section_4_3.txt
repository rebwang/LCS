4.3 Recommendation Engine & Evaluation (Yue Liang)

Completed:

- Implemented the LCS-based recommendation function using weighted voting from the top-K most similar neighbours.
- Implemented a rating-aware prediction equation that incorporates neighbour similarity, recency, rating strength, and a small popularity prior.
- Implemented a random baseline recommender that selects N unseen movies at random.
- Implemented evaluation metrics including Hit Rate, Mean Reciprocal Rank (MRR), Precision@N, Recall@N, and NDCG@N for N in {1, 5, 10, 20}.
- Implemented the full evaluation loop that compares the LCS-based recommender with the random baseline across all test users.

Results:

Final experimental results on the filtered MovieLens dataset:

- Hit Rate@1: 0.0200
- Hit Rate@5: 0.0300
- Hit Rate@10: 0.0500
- Hit Rate@20: 0.0500
- MRR: 0.0248
- NDCG@10: 0.0304

For comparison, the random baseline achieved:

- Hit Rate@20: 0.0100
- MRR: 0.0007

Summary:

The LCS-based recommendation engine outperformed the random baseline on the main ranking metrics. The final version performed better after introducing rating-aware scoring and focusing on recent high-rating interactions, which made the recommendation results more relevant for next-item prediction.
